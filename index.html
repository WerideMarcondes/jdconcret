Why order a line pump?

It is primarily indicated for projects that require flexibility, precision in hard-to-reach locations, or for small to medium volumes of concrete. Unlike a boom pump, a line pump uses flexible hoses that can be installed through buildings, narrow alleys, or inside houses.

    Why order a Boom pump?

    It is essential for projects that require speed, vertical/horizontal reach, and efficiency in concreting, especially in large-scale or difficult-to-access projects.
